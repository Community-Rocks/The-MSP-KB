# Social Media communities

MSP professionals gather in a number of Facebook and LinkedIn groups to swap advice, share resources, and keep up with industry trends.

| Community | Platform | Focus | Link |

| **Everything MSP** (Dan Tomaszewski) | Facebook | Technology, business growth, and industry trends for MSPs. | [Visit](https://www.facebook.com/groups/everythingmsp/) |
